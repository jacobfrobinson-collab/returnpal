How referral rewards work

Use your unique referral link (or share by email). When someone signs up via your link, they appear here as Signed up until they send their first package. When they send their first package, they become Active and count toward your tier. Rewards use your current tier (based on how many active referrals you have).

Rewards are tiered: the more active referrals you have, the more you earn per referral. No limit on how many people you refer. Full terms apply.

Tier 1 (1–5 active): £10 each Tier 2 (6–10 active): £15 each Tier 3 (11+ active): £20 each
